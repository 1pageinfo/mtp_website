Applications of Pipes, Tubes, Fittings, Flanges & Bars

Oil & Gas Industry

Pipes, flanges and fittings are widely used in drilling, refineries and offshore platforms. They ensure safe transportation of crude oil, gas and petrochemical fluids.

Construction & Infrastructure

Steel tubes and structural pipes provide strength and durability for buildings, bridges and industrial infrastructure projects worldwide.

Power & Energy

High-pressure pipes and alloy fittings are used in thermal and renewable power plants to manage steam, heat transfer and energy generation systems.

Chemical & Petrochemical

Corrosion-resistant pipes and flanges safely handle aggressive chemicals, acids and high-temperature fluids in processing plants.

Marine & Shipbuilding

Stainless steel pipes and fittings resist seawater corrosion and ensure reliable performance in shipbuilding and offshore structures.

Automotive & Engineering

Precision tubes and alloy bars are widely used in automotive components, exhaust systems and heavy engineering applications.
