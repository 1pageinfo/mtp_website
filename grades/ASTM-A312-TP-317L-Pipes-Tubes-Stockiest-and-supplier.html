ASTM A312 TP 317L Pipes & Tubes Stockist and Supplier

ASTM A312 TP 317L Pipes & Tubes are manufactured from a low-carbon austenitic stainless steel grade designed for enhanced corrosion resistance in aggressive environments. The L grade indicates a lower carbon content, which minimizes carbide precipitation during welding and improves resistance to intergranular corrosion. This makes TP 317L pipes and tubes especially suitable for applications requiring extensive welding and long-term service reliability..

Compared to conventional stainless steel grades such as 316L, ASTM A312 TP 317L contains higher levels of chromium, nickel, and molybdenum. This chemical composition provides superior resistance to pitting, crevice corrosion, and chemical attack in chloride-rich and acidic environments. As a result, TP 317L Pipes & Tubes are widely used in chemical processing plants, petrochemical industries, marine systems, pulp & paper mills, and pollution control equipment..

ASTM A312 TP 317L Pipes & Tubes are available in both seamless and welded forms, including ERW and EFW, to meet diverse pressure, temperature, and dimensional requirements. These products are supplied in annealed and pickled condition as per ASTM A312 / ASME SA312 standards, ensuring consistent mechanical properties, excellent surface finish, and compliance with international quality norms..

With the ability to supply standard as well as customized dimensions, the company supports projects of all scales, from routine industrial piping to complex, high-corrosion applications worldwide..

What are ASTM A312 TP 317L Pipes & Tubes used for?

ASTM A312 TP 317L Pipes & Tubes are used in chemical processing, petrochemical plants, marine environments, pulp & paper mills, pharmaceutical industries, and pollution control systems where high corrosion resistance is required.

What is the advantage of TP 317L over TP 317?

TP 317L has a lower carbon content, which reduces the risk of intergranular corrosion after welding and improves performance in welded structures.

Are ASTM A312 TP 317L Pipes available in seamless and welded forms?

Yes, ASTM A312 TP 317L Pipes & Tubes are available in seamless, ERW, and EFW welded forms to suit different pressure and size requirements.

What certifications are provided with TP 317L pipes?
