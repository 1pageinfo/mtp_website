ASTM A335 P5 Pipes Stockist and Supplier

ASTM A335 P5 Pipes are seamless ferritic alloy steel pipes manufactured in accordance with ASTM A335/A335M standards and are specifically designed for high-temperature and high-pressure service. These pipes contain chromium and molybdenum alloying elements, which provide excellent resistance to oxidation, corrosion, and creep at elevated temperatures. ASTM A335 P5 Pipes are widely used in critical applications where strength and thermal stability are essential..

What does P5 mean in ASTM A335?

The designation P5 refers to a chromium-molybdenum alloy steel grade containing approximately 4/6% chromium and 0.45/0.65% molybdenum, designed for high-temperature service with improved creep and corrosion resistance.

Are ASTM A335 P5 Pipes available in different quality levels?

ASTM A335 P5 Pipes are supplied as per ASTM/ASME specifications and are typically provided in the normalized and tempered condition. Quality and testing requirements are defined within the ASTM A335 standard.

What manufacturing forms are available for ASTM A335 P5 Pipes?

ASTM A335 P5 Pipes are manufactured and supplied in seamless (SMLS) form only, as required by the ASTM A335 specification for high-temperature alloy steel pipes.
