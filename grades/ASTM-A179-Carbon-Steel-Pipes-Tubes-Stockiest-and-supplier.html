Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is ASTM A179 Carbon Steel Pipe used for?

ASTM A179 Carbon Steel Pipes & Tubes are primarily used in heat exchangers, condensers, and other heat transfer equipment. They are designed for efficient heat transfer under low to moderate pressure and temperature conditions.

Are ASTM A179 Pipes seamless or welded?

ASTM A179 Pipes & Tubes are manufactured only in seamless form using a cold drawn process, ensuring uniform wall thickness and excellent surface finish.

What makes ASTM A179 suitable for heat exchangers?

ASTM A179 has low carbon content, good thermal conductivity, and excellent formability, making it ideal for bending, expanding, and rolling operations required in heat exchanger and condenser applications.

Are ASTM A179 tubes supplied in annealed condition?

Yes, ASTM A179 Carbon Steel Tubes are supplied in an annealed condition to improve ductility, machinability, and overall performance in heat transfer systems.

Can ASTM A179 tubes be supplied in U-bent form?

Yes, ASTM A179 Carbon Steel Tubes can be supplied in straight lengths as well as U-bent form as per heat exchanger and condenser design requirements.

What size range is available for ASTM A179 Pipes & Tubes?

ASTM A179 Pipes & Tubes are commonly available in outside diameters ranging from ½” OD to 4” OD with various wall thicknesses as per application needs.

Are test certificates provided with ASTM A179 Pipes?

Yes, ASTM A179 Carbon Steel Pipes & Tubes are supplied with Mill Test Certificates (MTC) as per EN 10204 3.1, confirming chemical and mechanical properties.
