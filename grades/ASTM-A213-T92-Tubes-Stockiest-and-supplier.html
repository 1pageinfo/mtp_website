ASTM A213 T92 Tubes Stockiest and Supplier

ASTM A213 T92 Tubes are advanced chromium-molybdenum-vanadium alloy steel tubes engineered for high-temperature and high-pressure service. They are manufactured according to ASTM A213 / ASME SA213 standards and are primarily used in power plants, refineries, and petrochemical industries. T92 tubes are designed for critical applications such as superheaters, reheaters, and boilers, providing superior strength, thermal fatigue resistance, and oxidation resistance at elevated temperatures compared to earlier T91 and T22 grades..

The chemical composition of T92 tubes includes approximately 9% chromium, 0.5/1% molybdenum, and small additions of vanadium, niobium, and nitrogen, along with carefully controlled carbon, manganese, and silicon. This alloy composition significantly improves creep strength and high-temperature performance, making it ideal for ultra-supercritical steam applications and environments with high thermal stress..

Mechanically, ASTM A213 T92 Tubes offer exceptional tensile and yield strength, excellent hardness, and good elongation. Typical mechanical properties include a minimum yield strength of 620 MPa, tensile strength of 760/950 MPa, and elongation around 20%. These properties ensure that T92 tubes maintain dimensional stability, toughness, and reliability under extreme operating conditions..

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are ASTM A213 T92 Tubes used for?

ASTM A213 T92 Tubes are used in high-temperature and high-pressure applications such as boilers, superheaters, reheaters, and heat exchangers. They are widely applied in power plants, petrochemical industries, and refineries.

What does T92 mean in ASTM A213?

T92 is a high-performance chromium-molybdenum-vanadium alloy steel grade with ~9% chromium, 0.5/1% molybdenum, and small additions of vanadium, niobium, and nitrogen. It provides superior creep strength and oxidation resistance at elevated temperatures.

Are ASTM A213 T92 Tubes seamless?

Yes, ASTM A213 T92 Tubes are primarily manufactured in seamless (SMLS) form to ensure strength and reliability under high-temperature, high-pressure conditions.

What heat treatment is applied to T92 tubes?

T92 tubes are supplied in normalized and tempered condition to enhance mechanical properties, creep resistance, and toughness for high-temperature service.
