Its performance characteristics ensure durability and longevity even under variable environmental and service conditions, positioning it as a preferred choice for long-distance transmission systems.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is ASTM A335 P12 pipe used for?

ASTM A335 P12 Pipes are used for high-temperature and high-pressure applications including boilers, superheaters, heat exchangers, refineries, and petrochemical plants. Their chromium-molybdenum alloy ensures excellent strength and oxidation resistance at elevated temperatures.

What does P12 mean in ASTM A335?

The “P12” designation indicates a chromium-molybdenum alloy steel containing approximately 0.90–1.20% chromium and 0.85–1.05% molybdenum, designed for high-temperature service with improved creep strength and corrosion resistance.

Are ASTM A335 P12 Pipes seamless?

Yes, ASTM A335 P12 Pipes are supplied in seamless (SMLS) form only, as required by ASTM A335 for high-temperature alloy steel pipes.

What heat treatment is applied to P12 Pipes?

ASTM A335 P12 Pipes are supplied in normalized and tempered condition, ensuring uniform wall thickness, dimensional accuracy, and enhanced mechanical properties.
