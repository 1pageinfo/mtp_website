Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Hastelloy C276?

Hastelloy C276 is a nickel-molybdenum-chromium alloy known for its exceptional resistance to corrosion, pitting, crevice corrosion, and stress corrosion cracking in severe environments.

What are Hastelloy C276 pipes used for?

Hastelloy C276 pipes are widely used in chemical processing, oil & gas, petrochemical, pulp and paper, pollution control, pharmaceutical, and marine industries.

What standards are Hastelloy C276 pipes manufactured to?

They are commonly manufactured as per ASTM B622, ASTM B619, ASTM B626, and ASTM B622 standards, depending on seamless or welded pipe requirements.

Are Hastelloy C276 pipes available in seamless and welded form?

Yes, Hastelloy C276 pipes are available in both seamless and welded forms, offering flexibility for high-pressure and high-temperature applications.

What is the chemical composition of Hastelloy C276?

The alloy primarily contains nickel, molybdenum, chromium, iron, tungsten, and small amounts of carbon and manganese.

Does Hastelloy C276 resist acids?

Yes, it shows excellent resistance to sulfuric, hydrochloric, phosphoric, nitric acids, and chlorine-containing environments.

Can Hastelloy C276 pipes withstand high temperatures?

Hastelloy C276 pipes perform well at elevated temperatures and maintain mechanical strength even in extreme thermal conditions.

Are Hastelloy C276 pipes suitable for welding?

Yes, they have excellent weldability and can be welded using standard welding techniques without post-weld heat treatment.

What sizes are available for Hastelloy C276 pipes?

Pipes are available in a wide range of outer diameters, wall thicknesses, and custom lengths as per client requirements.

How are Hastelloy C276 pipes tested?

They undergo tests such as chemical analysis, mechanical testing, hydrostatic testing, eddy current testing, and non-destructive inspections.

Are Hastelloy C276 pipes corrosion resistant in seawater?

Yes, the alloy provides excellent resistance to seawater corrosion and marine atmospheric conditions.

Can Hastelloy C276 pipes be customized?

Yes, pipes can be customized in terms of size, thickness, surface finish, and length to meet specific project requirements.
