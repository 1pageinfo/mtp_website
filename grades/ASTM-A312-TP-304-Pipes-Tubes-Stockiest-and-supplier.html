ASTM A312 TP 304 Pipes & Tubes Stockiest and Supplier

ASTM A312 TP 304 Pipes & Tubes are high-quality austenitic stainless steel pipes widely used across industries due to their excellent corrosion resistance, durability, and high-temperature strength. TP 304 grade, also known as 304 stainless steel, contains 18/20% chromium and 8/10.5% nickel, providing outstanding resistance to oxidation and corrosion in a variety of environments, including chemical processing, food processing, pharmaceuticals, and marine applications.

These pipes and tubes are manufactured according to ASTM A312 standards, ensuring compliance with international specifications for seamless (SMLS) and welded (ERW, LSAW, SSAW) forms. They are available in a wide range of diameters, wall thicknesses, and lengths, making them suitable for pipelines, heat exchangers, condensers, and structural applications..

The mechanical properties of ASTM A312 TP 304 Pipes & Tubes make them strong, ductile, and easy to fabricate, which allows for bending, welding, and forming without cracking. Typical properties include a minimum yield strength of 215 MPa, tensile strength of 505/700 MPa, and elongation of =40%. These properties, combined with excellent impact resistance, make TP 304 ideal for industrial, petrochemical, and construction applications..

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are ASTM A312 TP 304 Pipes & Tubes used for?

ASTM A312 TP 304 Pipes & Tubes are primarily used for the transportation of water, chemicals, and other fluids in industrial, chemical, and food processing systems. They are widely applied in refineries, petrochemical plants, marine projects, and pharmaceutical plants due to their excellent corrosion resistance and durability.

What does TP 304 mean in ASTM A312?

The designation ?TP 304? refers to an austenitic stainless steel grade containing 18/20% chromium and 8/10.5% nickel. It provides excellent corrosion resistance, high ductility, and strength for a wide range of industrial applications.

Are ASTM A312 TP 304 Pipes & Tubes available in seamless and welded forms?

Yes, ASTM A312 TP 304 Pipes & Tubes are available in both seamless (SMLS) and welded forms, including ERW, LSAW, and SSAW, to suit different project requirements for diameter, pressure, and length.
