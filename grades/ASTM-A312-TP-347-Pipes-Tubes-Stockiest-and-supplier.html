ASTM A312 TP 347 Pipes & Tubes Stockist and Supplier

ASTM A312 TP 347 is a specification covering stainless steel seamless, welded, and heavily cold-worked pipes and tubes made from a niobium/stabilized austenitic stainless steel. This grade is defined under the industry standard ASTM A312 / ASME SA312, ensuring consistent quality and performance for high-temperature and corrosive environments. TP 347 is particularly valued for its ability to resist intergranular corrosion and to maintain strength in extreme heat, making it a preferred choice in demanding engineering applications..

The alloy composition of ASTM A312 TP 347 includes 17/20% chromium and 9/13% nickel, with additions of niobium (columbium) which stabilize the steel's microstructure. This stabilization reduces carbide precipitation during welding and high-temperature service, offering improved resistance to sensitization compared with unstabilized alloys. Mechanical properties include a minimum tensile strength of 515 MPa and a yield strength of 205 MPa, with excellent elongation for toughness and ductility..

One of the core strengths of TP 347 pipes and tubes is their wide range of applications. They are widely used in oil and gas processing, chemical and petrochemical industries, power generation, heat exchangers, pharmaceutical equipment, marine environments and high-temperature processing. Their heat and corrosion resistance also make them suitable for high-pressure steam and furnace components, where standard grades might fail.

From a form and supply perspective, ASTM A312 TP 347 is available in seamless, ERW (Electric Resistance Welded), and welded constructions, in sizes ranging from small bore tubes to large industrial pipe sizes, with various wall thickness schedules (e.g., SCH 10S, SCH 40S, SCH 80S). Tubes can also be supplied in polished, annealed, and pickled finishes and custom cut lengths to suit precise engineering requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is ASTM A312 TP 347 pipe used for?

ASTM A312 TP 347 Pipes & Tubes are primarily used in **chemical, petrochemical, power generation, and high-temperature applications**. They are ideal for transporting **corrosive fluids, steam, and gases**, and are widely applied in **refineries, heat exchangers, boilers, and industrial piping systems** due to their excellent corrosion resistance and high-temperature strength.

What does TP 347 mean in ASTM A312?

The designation ?TP 347? indicates a **stabilized austenitic stainless steel grade** containing niobium (columbium) to improve **creep and intergranular corrosion resistance**. It is suitable for welding and high-temperature service.

Is ASTM A312 TP 347 available in different specifications?

Yes, ASTM A312 TP 347 Pipes & Tubes are available in **both seamless (SMLS) and welded forms**. They meet stringent chemical composition and mechanical property requirements, making them suitable for **critical applications**.
