ASTM A312 TP 317 Pipes & Tubes Stockiest and Supplier

ASTM A312 TP 317 refers to a specific grade of austenitic stainless steel (UNS S31700 / Werkstoff 1.4449) specified under the ASTM A312 standard for seamless and welded pipes and tubes. This grade is distinguished by its elevated molybdenum, chromium, and nickel content, which significantly enhances its resistance to pitting, crevice corrosion, and chloride environments compared to standard 300-series grades. The pipes and tubes are supplied in annealed and pickled condition per ASTM requirements, and they adhere to strict quality benchmarks for mechanical and chemical properties.

ASTM A312 TP 317 products are available in a broad range of sizes, thicknesses, and schedules, typically from small bore to large diameters, in seamless, ERW, EFW, and fabricated forms. These pipes & tubes are suitable for high temperature and high corrosion environments, making them a versatile choice for demanding industrial applications. As a global supplier and stockist, Metal Tubular Products ensures that these items are readily stocked in standard and custom lengths, with fast delivery options worldwide.

Due to their robust corrosion resistance, ASTM A312 TP 317 Pipes & Tubes are widely used in sectors such as chemical processing, marine/offshore, pharmaceutical production, flue gas desulfurization systems, and pulp & paper plants. The enhanced alloy composition provides a stronger passive film and improved resistance against localized attack, especially in environments with chlorides, acids, and aggressive chemical media.

From a supply chain perspective, reliable stockists maintain a comprehensive inventory of seamless and welded pipe configurations, including special finishes such as polished or electropolished surfaces for critical applications. What are ASTM A312 TP 317 Pipes & Tubes used for?

ASTM A312 TP 317 Pipes & Tubes are used in highly corrosive environments such as chemical processing, petrochemical plants, marine applications, pulp & paper mills, pharmaceutical industries, and heat exchangers. What is TP 317 stainless steel?

TP 317 is an austenitic stainless steel grade under ASTM A312 with higher chromium, nickel, and molybdenum content compared to 316. This composition offers improved corrosion resistance in aggressive chemical and chloride-rich environments.

Are ASTM A312 TP 317 Pipes & Tubes available in seamless and welded forms?

Yes, ASTM A312 TP 317 Pipes & Tubes are available in seamless and welded forms, including ERW and EFW. These options allow flexibility based on pressure ratings, sizes, and specific project requirements.
