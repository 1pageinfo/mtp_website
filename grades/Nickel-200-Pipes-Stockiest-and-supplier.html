Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Nickel 200 Pipe used for?

Nickel 200 Pipes are used in chemical, petrochemical, and seawater applications, including heat exchangers, reactors, tanks, and piping systems that require resistance to caustic and acidic environments.

What makes Nickel 200 different from other nickel alloys?

Nickel 200 is essentially pure nickel with excellent corrosion resistance, high ductility, and good mechanical strength, making it ideal for harsh chemical and industrial environments.
