ASTM A335 P91 Pipes Stockiest and Supplier

ASTM A335 P91 Pipes are high-performance seamless alloy steel pipes manufactured in accordance with ASTM A335 / ASME SA335 standards and are specifically designed for high-temperature and high-pressure service. This grade contains chromium, molybdenum, vanadium, and niobium, which together provide exceptional creep strength, oxidation resistance, and long-term structural stability at elevated temperatures. ASTM A335 P91 Pipes are widely used in advanced power generation and critical industrial applications.

Metal Tubular Products is a worldwide stockist and supplier of ASTM A335 P91 Pipes, supplying premium-quality seamless pipes to power plants, refineries, petrochemical units, and oil & gas processing facilities. The company maintains a wide inventory covering various diameters, wall thicknesses, and lengths, enabling fast and reliable supply for both shutdown maintenance and large-scale EPC projects across global markets..

ASTM A335 P91 Pipes supplied by Metal Tubular Products offer superior mechanical properties compared to conventional chrome-moly grades. Their excellent creep rupture strength allows operation at higher temperatures and pressures, improving plant efficiency and service life. These pipes are commonly used in superheaters, reheaters, headers, steam pipelines, and high-temperature pressure parts.

All ASTM A335 P91 Pipes are manufactured using advanced seamless processes and are supplied in the normalized and tempered condition to achieve optimum microstructure and performance. Proper heat treatment ensures uniform wall thickness, dimensional accuracy, and enhanced weldability when appropriate welding procedures and post-weld heat treatment are followed..

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is ASTM A335 P91 pipe used for?

ASTM A335 P91 Pipes are used in high-temperature and high-pressure applications such as superheaters, reheaters, steam pipelines, headers, power plants, refineries, and petrochemical industries.

What does P91 mean in ASTM A335?

The P91 designation refers to a high-strength chromium-molybdenum-vanadium alloy steel designed for superior creep resistance and long-term performance at elevated temperatures.

Are ASTM A335 P91 Pipes seamless?

Yes, ASTM A335 P91 Pipes are supplied in seamless (SMLS) form only, as required by ASTM A335 for critical high-temperature applications.

What heat treatment is applied to P91 Pipes?

ASTM A335 P91 Pipes are supplied in normalized and tempered condition to achieve the required microstructure, strength, and creep resistance.
