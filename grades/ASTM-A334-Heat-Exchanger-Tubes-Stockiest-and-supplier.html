Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are ASTM A334 Heat Exchanger Tubes used for?

ASTM A334 Heat Exchanger Tubes are primarily used in heat exchangers, condensers, and piping systems operating at low and sub-zero temperatures. They are widely applied in refineries, petrochemical plants, LNG facilities, and cryogenic service due to their excellent low-temperature toughness.

What makes ASTM A334 suitable for low-temperature applications?

ASTM A334 tubes are manufactured with controlled chemical composition and undergo mandatory impact testing to ensure high notch toughness and resistance to brittle fracture at low temperatures.

Which grades are available in ASTM A334 Heat Exchanger Tubes?

ASTM A334 Heat Exchanger Tubes are available in multiple grades such as Grade 1, Grade 3, Grade 6, and Grade 9, with Grade 6 being the most commonly used.

What manufacturing forms are available for ASTM A334 Heat Exchanger Tubes?

ASTM A334 Heat Exchanger Tubes are manufactured in seamless form only, ensuring uniform strength and reliability for heat exchanger and low-temperature service.

Are ASTM A334 Heat Exchanger Tubes impact tested?

Yes, impact testing is mandatory for ASTM A334 Heat Exchanger Tubes to verify their toughness and performance at specified low temperatures.
