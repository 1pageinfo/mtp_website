Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Super Duplex S32550 Pipe used for?

S32550 Pipes are used for transporting oil, gas, water, and chemical fluids in pipelines. They are suitable for offshore, desalination, and chemical plant applications due to their high strength and corrosion resistance.

What makes S32550 different from other duplex grades?

Super Duplex S32550 has a higher chromium, molybdenum, and nitrogen content than standard duplex grades, providing superior resistance to pitting, crevice corrosion, and stress corrosion cracking.

What manufacturing forms are available for S32550 Pipes?

S32550 Pipes are available in Seamless (SMLS), ERW, LSAW, and SSAW forms, allowing flexibility in size, pressure rating, and project requirements.

What is the supply condition of S32550 Pipes?

S32550 Pipes are supplied in PSL 1 and PSL 2, meeting international standards with mandatory testing and quality control for critical applications.
