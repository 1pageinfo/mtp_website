Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are Duplex Steel S32205 Pipes used for?

S32205 Pipes are widely used for oil, gas, water, and chemical transportation, as well as for offshore and desalination projects due to their excellent corrosion resistance and strength.

What is special about S32205 compared to other stainless steels?

S32205 has a duplex microstructure with higher chromium, molybdenum, and nitrogen content, providing superior resistance to pitting, crevice corrosion, and stress corrosion cracking.

What forms are available for S32205 Pipes?

Duplex S32205 Pipes are available in Seamless (SMLS), ERW, LSAW, and SSAW, suitable for a wide range of pressure ratings and diameters.

What supply conditions are available?

S32205 Pipes are supplied in PSL 1 and PSL 2, adhering to international standards with mandatory testing and quality control.
