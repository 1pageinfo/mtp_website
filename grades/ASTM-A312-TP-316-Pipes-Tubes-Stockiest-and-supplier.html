Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is API 5L X42 pipe used for?

ASTM A312 TP 316 Pipes & Tubes are primarily used for the transportation of oil, gas, water, and other fluids in pipeline systems. They are widely applied in onshore and offshore projects, refineries, petrochemical plants, and water transmission lines due to their good strength and weldability.

What does X42 mean in API 5L?

The designation “X42” refers to the minimum yield strength of the pipe, which is 42,000 psi (290 MPa). Higher X numbers indicate higher yield strength.

Is API 5L X42 available in PSL 1 and PSL 2?

Yes, ASTM A312 TP 316 Pipes & Tubes are available in both PSL 1 and PSL 2. PSL 2 has stricter chemical composition limits, mandatory impact testing, and enhanced quality control compared to PSL 1.

What manufacturing forms are available for ASTM A312 TP 316 Pipes & Tubes?

ASTM A312 TP 316 Pipes & Tubes are manufactured in various forms such as Seamless (SMLS), ERW, LSAW, and SSAW, allowing flexibility for different pressure, diameter, and project requirements.
