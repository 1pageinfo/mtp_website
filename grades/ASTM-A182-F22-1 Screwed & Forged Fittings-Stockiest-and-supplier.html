This standard covers forged fittings such as elbows, tees, reducers, caps, and couplings, as well as screwed fittings with threaded connections. Both types are designed to withstand high pressure and temperature in critical industrial piping systems.

ASTM A182 F22 fittings are made from chromium-molybdenum steel containing carbon, manganese, phosphorus, sulfur, silicon, chromium, and molybdenum. This ensures high strength, excellent heat resistance, and moderate corrosion resistance for demanding industrial applications.
