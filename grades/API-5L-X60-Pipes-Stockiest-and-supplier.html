API 5L X60 Pipes Stockiest and Supplier

API 5L X60 Pipes are high-strength line pipes manufactured in accordance with the American Petroleum Institute (API) Specification 5L and are widely used for the transportation of oil, natural gas, water, and other fluids in pipeline systems. The X60 grade offers a minimum yield strength of 60,000 PSI, making it suitable for medium to high-pressure applications where reliability, safety, and long-term performance are critical. These pipes are commonly used in onshore and offshore pipeline projects across the energy and infrastructure sectors..

Metal Tubular Products is a worldwide stockist and supplier of API 5L X60 Pipes, providing high-quality products that meet international standards and project-specific requirements. The company supplies API 5L X60 Pipes in seamless and welded forms, including ERW, LSAW, and SSAW, with a wide range of diameters, wall thicknesses, and lengths. This flexibility allows Metal Tubular Products to support small-scale installations as well as large EPC and pipeline projects globally..

API 5L X60 Pipes supplied by Metal Tubular Products are known for their excellent mechanical properties, good weldability, and resistance to deformation under high pressure. The controlled chemical composition ensures consistent performance and reduces the risk of cracking or failure during operation. These characteristics make API 5L X60 Pipes ideal for long-distance transmission pipelines and demanding environmental conditions..

To enhance durability and service life, API 5L X60 Pipes can be supplied with various surface finishes and protective coatings such as black varnish, Fusion Bonded Epoxy (FBE), 3LPE, and 3LPP. These coatings provide effective protection against corrosion, moisture, and chemical exposure, making the pipes suitable for underground, subsea, and exposed applications..
