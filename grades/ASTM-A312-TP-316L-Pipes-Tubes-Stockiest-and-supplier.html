ASTM A312 TP 316L Pipes & Tubes are premium austenitic stainless steel pipes and tubes designed for superior corrosion resistance, especially against chlorides, marine environments, and chemical processing applications. Grade 316L is a low-carbon version of 316, which minimizes carbide precipitation during welding, ensuring excellent corrosion resistance in welded structures and high-temperature environments..

Metal Tubular Products is a global stockist and supplier of ASTM A312 TP 316L Pipes & Tubes, providing seamless and welded options to meet a wide range of industrial applications. Their products are manufactured according to ASTM A312 / ASME SA312 standards and undergo strict quality control to ensure durability, uniformity, and traceability for international projects..

ASTM A312 TP 316L Pipes & Tubes are widely used in chemical processing, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, marine, and industrial piping systems, where long-term resistance to corrosion and high hygiene standards are required. The addition of molybdenum enhances resistance to pitting and crevice corrosion, making them ideal for chloride-rich or acidic environments..

These stainless steel pipes and tubes are available in seamless (SMLS) and welded (ERW) forms with a variety of schedules, diameters, wall thicknesses, and end finishes to suit different project specifications.
